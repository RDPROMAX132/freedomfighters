Savitribai Phule

Born on January 3, 1831, Savitribai Jyoti Rao Phule emerged as a prominent reformer, educationalist, and poet in Maharashtra. India’s first female teacher, she actively advanced women’s rights alongside her husband, taking the initiative to establish the first modern Indian girls school in Pune. She passed away on March 10, 1897 in Pune
